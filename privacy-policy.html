Privacy Policy — Timesheet Basic

Last updated: June 15, 2026

1. Who we are

  • Vendor: Dev labs
  • Contact: pritampawar.dev@gmail.com

2. Scope

This policy applies only to data processed by Timesheet Basic in Jira Cloud.

3. Data we process

To provide timesheet functionality, the app processes:

  • Jira worklog data (time spent, dates, and worklog text if present)
  • Jira issue identifiers and metadata needed for worklog context
  • Jira user account identifiers and display names for personal and team views

4. How we use data

  • Display personal and team timesheets
  • Enable users to create, update, and delete Jira worklogs
  • Support CSV export of user-visible timesheet data

5. Legal basis and permissions

Processing occurs based on customer/admin installation and user interaction in Jira, using Forge and Jira OAuth scopes granted during installation.

6. Data storage and retention

  • Primary system of record remains Jira.
  • The app uses Jira APIs for functionality and does not require external analytics or ad tracking.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data and we do not share customer data with advertisers. Data may be processed by Atlassian infrastructure as part of Forge platform operation.

8. International transfers

Data handling may involve Atlassian cloud regions according to Atlassian platform operations and customer configuration.

9. Security

We use Atlassian Forge platform controls and least-privilege scopes required for functionality.

10. Your rights

Data subjects should use organization admin channels and Atlassian/Jira controls for access, correction, export, and deletion requests related to Jira data.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time and will update the date above for material changes.

12. Contact
